ABOUT

Thank you for visiting my page. My name is Anna Sanderson and I'm a web developer. I am originally from Washington State and currently live in Florida. In a former life, I served in the United States Air Force for four years and was in the financial services industry for ten years. In April of 2016, I decided to enroll in Genaral Assembly's Web Development Immersive program. Since graduation, I have had the privilage of working on a website for a local logistics company, have built a few projects in my spare time, and participated in a QA project for General Assembly's revamped courses.

If you click on "Portfolio" on the menu bar or scroll down, you will be taken to a sampling of some of my projects. The Tic Tac Toe game was built with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. The hover image is of a Dalek because I am a Doctor Who fan. The second project is called Recipe Saver. This app was made using Ruby on Rails, CSS, HTML, Materialize, and jQuery. It enables users to save and add their recipes. This app is also mobile friendly.
